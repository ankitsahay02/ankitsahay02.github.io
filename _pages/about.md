Hi everybody! My name is Ankit Sahay, I am a PhD candidate working in [Dr. Sujith's](http://www.ae.iitm.ac.in/~sujith/) lab at [IIT Madras](https://www.iitm.ac.in/). My research lies is at the intersection of complex networks and thermoacoustic instability. Specifically, I focus on the investigations of large coherent structures (vortices) in turbulent combustors using complex networks approach. This work is motivated by the contribution of large coherent structures towards thermoacoustic instabilities exhibited by combustion systems such as rocket engines and gas turbines. Visualizing, analyzing and controlling these structures in lab-scale turbulent combustors is at the core of my work. I also study how complex networks can be used to improve our understanding of large-scale phenomena such as floods, climate changes, etc.

My interests outside research include cycling and reading classics. My Goodreads profile can be accessed [here](https://www.goodreads.com/user/show/60600126-ankit-sahay).
