My research lies is at the intersection of complex networks and thermoacoustic instability. Specifically, I focus on the investigations of large coherent structures (vortices) in turbulent combustors using complex networks approach. This work is motivated by the contribution of large coherent structures towards thermoacoustic instabilities exhibited by combustion systems such as rocket engines and gas turbines. Experimentally controlling, visualizing and analyzing these structures in lab-scale turbulent combustors is at the core of my work. I also study how  complex networks can be used to improve our understanding of large-scale phenomena such as floods, climate changes, etc.
